# 🩺 Insurance Cost Prediction Dataset

## 📌 Overview
This dataset is adapted from the book *Machine Learning with R* by Brett Lantz. It contains information about individuals and their medical insurance charges. The original datasets are publicly available but required cleaning and formatting to align with the book's examples.

This dataset is commonly used for regression tasks, especially to predict medical insurance costs based on personal attributes.

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following columns:

- **age**  
  Age of the primary beneficiary.

- **sex**  
  Gender of the insurance contractor (`female`, `male`).

- **bmi**  
  Body Mass Index (BMI), calculated as weight (kg) / height (m²).  
  - Ideal range: 18.5 – 24.9

- **children**  
  Number of dependents covered by health insurance.

- **smoker**  
  Smoking status (`yes`, `no`).

- **region**  
  Residential area in the US:
  - northeast
  - southeast
  - southwest
  - northwest

- **charges**  
  Individual medical costs billed by health insurance.
